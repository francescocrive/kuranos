Wearable device & database to track Parkinson's symptoms

Helping the world to know more about Parkinson's

"Knowledge is power", said F. Bacon in 16th century, and this remains true today in the world of Internet of Things, Big Data and Machine Learning. There are 7 million Parkinson's patients in the world today who cannot accurately track their disease evolution and the efficacy of their treatments.
KURANOS' goal is to solve this, enabling constant monitoring of the disease, treatment customization and quality of life improvement.
